ABOUT TXC

At TXC, we live and breathe street culture. Our store was founded by a collective of sneakerheads, creators, and style enthusiasts who believe that fashion is more than just clothing—it's a statement, an attitude, and a lifestyle. We curate the freshest drops, exclusive collaborations, and timeless staples from the world’s most sought-after streetwear brands, as well as up-and-coming local labels.

OUR MISSION

TXC is dedicated to empowering individuality and creative self-expression. We strive to provide our community with the latest trends, high-quality apparel, and a seamless shopping experience. Whether you’re chasing the next big thing or building your own signature look, our diverse collection has something for every taste and vibe.
